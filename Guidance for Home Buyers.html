1. Before Buying the Property - Key Points to Consider

Finding Project on MahaRERA: Use the Project search feature on MahaRERA to find registered projects.

Researching about the Property: Investigate important aspects such as title, approvals, common amenities, and services.

Agreement Stage: Understand the terms and conditions outlined in the agreement.

2. After Owning a Property

Tracking Progress: Monitor the project's completion, obtain necessary NOCs, and track approval stages.

Monitoring Quarterly Compliances: Keep an eye on quarterly compliances to ensure adherence to regulations.

Project Validity: Verify the validity of the project's approvals and documents.

3. Possession Stage Guidance

Before taking possession, ensure all your documents are in place and check for the following important parameters and documentations:

1. OC Status

Check if the Occupancy Certificate (OC) has been issued by the civic authority, indicating compliance with approved plans and building laws.

2. Architectâ€™s Completion Certificate (Form 4)

Verify if the promoter has uploaded Form 4, issued by the architect upon receipt of the OC, on the MahaRERA portal.

3. Amenities

Inspect all promised amenities to ensure they are in place and usable. Check project work status on MahaRERA portal for any delays.

4. Internal Work

Fittings

Check switchboards, doorknobs, bathroom fittings, etc., ensuring they match the agreed specifications.

Doors and Windows

Ensure all doors and windows comply with sanctioned plans and are of satisfactory quality.

5. Workmanship Defects

Inspect for cracks, holes, or deformations in the construction.

6. Drainage Outlets and Leakages

Check drainage outlets for clogs and inspect taps, faucets, and sinks for leakages.

7. Electrical Fittings

Verify the functionality of switches, plug-points, and MCBs, ensuring they meet quality standards.

8. Size of the Rooms

Measure room dimensions to ensure compliance with the Sale Agreement and sanctioned plans.
